## Design Context

### Users
EventHex feature pages are primarily for event organizers, operations teams, and B2B buyers evaluating product capabilities. These pages need to explain the workflow clearly, support product comparison, and convert visitors into demo or inquiry leads.

### Brand Personality
Trusted, modern, AI-first. The experience should feel credible and product-led rather than experimental or heavily stylized.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep the existing Astro feature-page visual language established by the current EventHex pages, especially the hero, bento/showcase, split content sections, FAQs, and CTA rhythm already used in the live feature pages. When migrating pages from the old frontend, preserve the existing content and adapt it into this Astro pattern rather than redesigning the content structure from scratch.

### Design Principles
- Reuse the current Astro feature-page pattern before inventing new layouts.
- Preserve old feature-page content, hierarchy, and intent unless there is a clear gap.
- If the old page has extra sections, redesign those sections to match the current Astro system rather than dropping them.
- Keep pages conversion-friendly with clear CTAs and WordPress-compatible content/media flows.
- Prioritize responsive, production-safe implementations over visual experimentation.
